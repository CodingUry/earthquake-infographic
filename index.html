WHAT TO DO IN AN EARTHQUAKE

Earthquakes, a common natural disaster worldwide, are caused by a sudden release of energy on Earth's crust, resulting in seismic waves that can cause significant damage. The aftermath can be devastating, with secondary effects such as landslides, tsunamis, and building collapses leading to considerable casualties. Given the unpredictability, and potential devastation of earthquakes, it's crucial for everyone, regardless of where they live, to be prepared. This guide will provide comprehensive advice on what to do before, during, and after an earthquake, applicable to any location worldwide
